Your Guide to Servicing The IPHEC Contract

LeadIT Corporation is proud to be a qualified provider of Information Technology Personnel and Consulting Services under the IPHEC (Illinois Public Higher Education Cooperative) contract, serving the University of Illinois System and other participating public higher education institutions across the state.

About Our
Company Freedom to
do more

With over 11 years of experience in IT consulting and services, LeadIT has built long-standing partnerships by delivering technology solutions that are both competitive and technically advanced. This success is powered by a team of 250+ consultants across a wide range of skillsets—90% of whom are on our W2—bringing expertise and strategic vision to every engagement.

We serve more than 22 direct clients and 85+ IT staffing partners, providing services to Fortune 500 companies, government agencies, and educational institutions. Through 150 affiliate partners, our network extends to over 350 clients and 500+ consultants.

IT Planning & Strategy

We are committed to working closely with the University’s stakeholders to create a comprehensive IT strategy plan. This includes bringing expertise, process and tools and providing consultancy on legislative requirements, regulatory health, safety and environmental obligations and issues which may apply on University campuses.

Information Security

We are committed to ensuring students and faculty are provided with the most reliable and secure access to equipment, systems and networks owned or operated by the University and its campuses.

Software/Application Development Services

Using agile processes, we provide software design, development and deployment services for customized administrative systems, learning management systems, teaching systems, and software for research.Our student management and student information solutions will help align the university and its campuses with emerging technologies and leverage them to improve the student and faculty experience and enable the University to replicate its success across campuses.

Teaching and Learning Services

Lead IT provides a range of instructional support services that include assisting faculty and students with the use of available technologies, creating on-demand training and e-learning modules across domains.

Mobile Technology Services

Our mobile technology and app development expertise across platforms will help staff and students to have access to University apps, data, study material, media and the Internet via a wide range of devices. We also provide support for third party apps that aid studies or staff work

Web Content & Development Services

We have expert designers and developers who can write clear, robust, well-documented code to build secure, accessible, and fast websites or improve on existing websites or review them for functionality, accessibility, security, performance, and maintainability

Desktop Services

Our desktop support teams can help reduce infrastructure and management costs by providing a range of managed services to support individual desktops making expert use of centralized application and filestore servers and cloud services.

Networking Services

Our network team assumes responsibility for maintaining and developing the University’s data network, wireless services and telecoms infrastructure. This includes: network monitoring and security, providing short term support for events and conferences, and provision of local internet access.

Infrastructure Services

We detect, fix and report on vulnerabilities and advise on security architecture, s/w acquisition, and network design to protect the University’s data and ensure compliance with security regulations, policies, and standards.

Customer Service Services

Lead IT hires efficient, effective resources to provide customer care in a professional and courteous manner and to respond to communications, by letter, email, or phone-call, promptly and courteously.
As part of the IPHEC contract, we welcome the opportunity to collaborate with Illinois public higher education institutions. We are ready to deliver cost-effective, efficient, and professionally executed technology services tailored to your needs. We look forward to receiving your requirements and quote requests in any of the service categories. LeadIT is here to support your mission with trusted resources and solutions.
